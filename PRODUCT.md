# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Potential clients, recruiters, and creative peers.

## Product Purpose
An interactive CV and portfolio website designed to showcase coding talent, skillset, and work ethic. The application itself is the primary proof of technical capability and creativity.

## Brand Personality
Technical, obsessive, experimental.

## Anti-references
- Overly chaotic "Awwwards-style" WebGL soup (e.g., giant floating 3D hands, messy wireframe blobs, chaotic overlapping typography).
- Cluttered, overly-complex experimental interfaces that sacrifice basic usability and reading for pure aesthetic novelty.

## Design Principles
- **The Medium is the Message:** The portfolio app itself must perform flawlessly and look meticulously crafted to demonstrate engineering talent and work ethic.
- **Experimental but Legible:** Push technical boundaries (e.g., data viz, AI agents, brutalist layout) without devolving into chaotic, unusable Awwwards layouts.
- **Technical Precision:** Typography, spacing, and interactions should feel deliberate, obsessive, and highly engineered.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
No specific constraints; baseline usability applies.
