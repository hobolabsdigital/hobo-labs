## The Problem
Traditional vehicle search filters are sterile, confusing, and fail to provide a seamless transition between inspiration and the final purchase.

## The Solution
Architected a Micro-Frontend Vue application integrating a lightning-fast Elastic Search backend to handle real-time inventory filtering and location-based matching.

## Overview & Impact
Replaced traditional sterile search filters with an intuitive, playful dialogue that elegantly guides users. Search results update instantaneously with every input, showcasing accessory customizations and exactly which vehicles are locally available with precise pricing. The platform became a benchmark for customer-oriented online automotive selling.
