## The Problem
Content production was bogged down by expensive, manual processes and monolithic architectures that struggled to scale efficiently during peak loads.

## The Solution
Designed a multi-agent AI operating system on GCP that automates end-to-end content production. The system employs a highly resilient hub-and-spoke architecture featuring 'Herman' (the orchestrator) and distributed serverless GPU workers.

## Overview & Impact
Hermes revolutionized the production workflow by treating AI agents as modular workers within a serverless ecosystem. By decoupling the orchestrator from the GPU-intensive rendering tasks, we drastically reduced idle time and overhead. The architecture achieved a 95% cost reduction while dramatically increasing throughput, consistency, and reliability across the platform.
