## The Problem
In spring 2021, public exhibitions were still not permitted due to pandemic restrictions. After the decision was made to hold the Global Innovation Summit as a digital event, it was agreed that the exhibition showcasing 16 selected innovations would take place in a virtual format.

## The Solution
Within just two months, the team brought an entire virtual exhibition to life. An immersive 3D environment was created where organizations and projects were showcased. Visitors could explore the space freely via their web browser or follow a guided tour with virtual hosts Jack and Lydia. Despite the tight development timeline, the experience was fully optimized for smooth performance on mobile devices.

## Overview & Impact
The interactive 3D exhibition was built with Nuxt, Strapi, and Three.js, delivering an immersive web experience for the SFG and FFG. The virtual exhibition attracted over 3,500 global visitors and offered a unique 10-minute guided tour through 16 innovations. The platform demonstrated that virtual exhibitions could be fast, interactive, and easy to navigate — offering users a new perspective on the future. Client: look! design / SFG / FFG.
