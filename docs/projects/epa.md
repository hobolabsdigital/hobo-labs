## The Problem
Handling highly sensitive medical data often results in clunky, unapproachable user interfaces that discourage active patient participation and adherence to medical regimens.

## The Solution
Engineered an advanced digital health application that extends the standard German Electronic Patient Record (ePA) with powerful, user-centric features natively on iOS using SwiftUI.

## Overview & Impact
Designed, developed, and maintained complex UI architectures and workflows utilizing modern SwiftUI, ensuring an accessible, highly responsive, and strictly secure patient user experience. The ePA+ ecosystem goes far beyond basic document storage, including a comprehensive Health Diary, Preventive Care Manager, and Vaccination Recommendations. It meets the strict regulatory and security specifications mandated by 'gematik'.
