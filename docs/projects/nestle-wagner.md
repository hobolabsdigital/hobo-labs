## The Problem
Revitalizing a traditional brand requires breaking through social media noise, especially among younger consumers, without forcing them to download a dedicated app.

## The Solution
Developed a modular SocialAR campaign containing nine distinct AR experiences published natively to Instagram and TikTok, merging physical product packaging with digital interactions.

## Overview & Impact
Engineered 'hero games' where users controlled the gameplay entirely via facial expressions (winking and nodding). We built an image tracker that recognized the physical pizza boxes in real-time, unlocking surprising Easter eggs directly in the supermarket aisle. The frictionless, no-app entry point led to massive viral sharing.
