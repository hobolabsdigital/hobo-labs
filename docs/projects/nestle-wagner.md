# Wagner BIG CITY Pizza (Nestlé)

## Overview

A modular SocialAR campaign designed to revitalize Wagner's BIG CITY Pizza brand and increase social media reach among young consumers. We created nine distinct AR experiences published directly to Instagram and TikTok, merging physical product packaging with engaging digital interactions to generate over a million impressions.

## Tech Stack

- SocialAR (Instagram & TikTok Filters)
- Facial Recognition & Tracking (Wink/Nod controls)
- Image Tracking (Physical Object Recognition)

## My Role & Contributions

- **AR Experience Developer:** Developed nine modular AR experiences across three publication waves, scaling rapidly to represent different target cities.
- **Game Mechanics & Interaction:** Engineered "hero games" where users controlled the gameplay entirely via facial expressions (winking and nodding) to collect pizza slice power-ups and chase high scores.
- **Physical-to-Digital Bridge:** Programmed an image tracker that recognized the physical pizza boxes in real-time, unlocking surprising Easter eggs for customers directly in the supermarket aisle.

## Interesting Details

- **No-App Frictionless Entry:** The experiences were published natively on Instagram and TikTok, eliminating the barrier of an app download and encouraging rapid viral sharing.
- **Face-Controlled Gameplay:** The hero games didn't use touch; they uniquely utilized facial motion tracking (winking/nodding) to make the experience playful, intuitive, and highly shareable as selfies.
- **Supermarket Surprises:** The pizza box became a digital gateway. Scanning the packaging anywhere—even in the store—activated the SocialAR effects.
- **High Engagement:** Since its launch in July 2020, the filters and games garnered over 1,000,000 impressions and more than 20,000 documented user reactions and gameplay clips.
