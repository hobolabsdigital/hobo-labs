Refined Design: The Impeccable Reviewer

Incorporating the review process, layout heuristics, and animations.

1. The "Impeccable" Critique Loop

The bees retain their mischievous nature! But instead of just injecting random chaos, they review their chaos.

We will literally steal the rules from the impeccable skill in Antigravity and put them in the AI prompt. The AI's thought process will be:

  1. Look at the current styles of a node.
  2. Critique it against the Impeccable laws (e.g. "This font is too generic, the contrast is too low").
  3. Output the fixed CSS variables in the JSON payload to "hack" the node into a premium editorial state.
2. Smart Heuristics for Layout

We will upgrade the float_nodes mischief. Instead of just randomly nudging nodes out of alignment, the "Smart Heuristics" will calculate a strict mathematical grid or alignment target, and the bees will push the nodes toward that perfect alignment.

3. Playground Override Controls

Right now, clicking a behavior in the playground just sets the "mood" for the next 60-second brain tick. We will add:

  • A "Force Brain Tick" button to immediately trigger the AI.
  • An "Override AI" toggle so you can lock the swarm into a specific behavior, preventing the AI from changing its mind.
4. The "Collapse" Animation

When a node is targeted by a theme_hack, we will use Framer Motion to give visual feedback:

The text content inside the node will rapidly scale down/collapse to 0, the new Impeccable CSS variables will be applied, and then the text will spring back to full size in the new font and color.
